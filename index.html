How KidAffinity works

KidAffinity has an extensive database of psychologically verified audio-visual and physical cues and stimuli. These images, videos, sounds and feelings are displayed to your child through your device’s screen, speakers and haptic touch technology.

Every young child has intrinsic and hormonal reactions to certain stimuli and cues that excite them. KidAffinity was built particularly with this in mind, using the highest standards of computing technology.
